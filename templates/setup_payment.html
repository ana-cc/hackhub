Setup Payment

We accept payments to our bank account via standing order.

We're still working out the exact details of how to handle these payments. The current plan involves manually checking the accont for incoming payments and then recording them so there will be a delay between your payment hitting our account and that being reflected here in hackhub. We intend to do checks of the account at least twice a month, around the middle and end of the month.

Money landing in our account without a reference can't be associated with a member so will be considered a donation. Transfers to cover membership fees must include your username as the reference.

Details

We have a handy form you can print, fill in your details on the dotted lines and give to your bank to set up the standing order. Please let us know if your bank has any problems with this form and we can hopefully get them ironed out.
